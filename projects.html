AspectHomes

The AspectHomes Project is our final Year project, which is based on customer and designer interactions. In the project, there are three modules: Admin, User, and Designer.

Admin can check all activities of User & Designer.

Tech Stack:
 HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, Python, Django

GitHub Live Demo

Description

User:

• Users begin by uploading reference images and 360-degree virtual tours of their living spaces, accompanied by their budget preferences. They explore a gallery of completed projects and 360-degree tours uploaded by designers, gaining insights into their creative prowess.

• Users can propose their project budgets, initiating an exciting phase where designers actively bid on their projects, bringing forth a diverse range of ideas.

Designer:

• Designers, in turn, have the opportunity to showcase their talents by uploading images and 360-degree virtual tours of their completed projects

• They eagerly participate in the bidding process, demonstrating their expertise and creativity to secure projects that align with their skills.

• The main part of the project involves the projects and photos uploaded by the user and designer, which will be available in 360 degrees. The 360-degree functionality will be implemented with the help of HTML and CSS.





Smart Vaccum Cleaner

The Arduino Uno 3 based smart vacuum cleaner is an innovative and intelligent cleaning solution for homes. It is a device that simplifies the job of keeping your home clean. This compact and versatile vacuum cleaner comes with advanced features and automation possibilities thanks to its integration with the Arduino Uno 3 microcontroller.

Tech Stack:
 C, C++, Java, AudrinoUno, Microcontroller

GitHub Live Demo

Description

Automation

Leveraging the Arduino Uno 3 microcontroller, this smart vacuum cleaner offers automated scheduling and customizable cleaning patterns for efficient and effortless home maintenance.

Intelligent Navigation

Equipped with advanced sensors, it detects obstacles and navigates various surfaces, ensuring thorough and effective cleaning throughout your space.

Compact Design

Its sleek, space-saving design makes it suitable for both small and large areas, combining modern technology with practicality for a cleaner home.
